## task 3
要点：
1. 阅读（15s）：一个建议、政策；两个原因或改变
2. 听力（5s+20s+20s）：一个态度，两个评价（举例细节）
3. 记笔记用简写符号（因果、列举、转折、假设）


模板

The school plans to ... because ... and ...

The man in the conversation disagrees/agrees with the proposal/policy.
Firstly, ...
Secondly, ...

## task 5
要点：
1. 听力：一个problem，两个solution
2. 30s客观陈述、30s个人观点
3. 记录十字法
4. 个人观点：X的好和Y的不好。优缺点要是听力原文的延伸
				 
模板：
In the conversation, the man/woman has a problem that ...
There're two solutions to solve the problem. The first one is to ... . The second one is to ... .

Personally speaking, I think the ... solution is better, because I

### 细节
1. 很忙
She is very busy at this moment, but doing sth will cost her a lot of time. In this case, this tight schedule will make her feel, you know, stressed out and burned out. In the end, probably, she can't do anything right.
2. 很贵
I think xxx is quite expensive for her. I mean, she is just a student who doesn't have regular income, so she doesn't have the financial ability to afford it. It is a huge burden for her. She cannot take it.

3. 很拥挤吵闹
... is full of people and noise, so it's not a good place for her to study. She cannot be totally focused in this kind of noisy and crowded environment. As a result, she can't get high quality in her study.

### 要点：
1. 语音语调。用conversational tone，说话要尽可能的清楚、流利。
2. TSE结构：1句topic sentence，1~2个reason，example。
3. example可以从以下几个方面补充：
	* when where who what
	* why
4. 万能模板

With the quick pace of people's life, people have less time to pay attention to their healthy diet, especially young people. For example. young people often eat some fast food in order to save time, such as French Fries, hamburgers, fried chicken, you know, something like that. Delicious, em..., but they are really harmful to their health. I think people should pay more attention to the healthy diet. For instance, we can eat some healthy food such as vegetables, fishes, soup and so on. After all, health is the most important thing in our daily life.



# 写作
## 综合写作
要点：
1. 找因果关系：
	1. 阅读：先果后因
	2. 听力：先因后果
2. 基本描述：
	The passage and the lecturer have a contradictory opinions on ...
	The passage/author suggests/claims/says
	On the contrary/However, the professor/lecturer argues/disagrees/opposes that ...
	
## 独立写作
自己捉摸一个模板，别套用别人的。
原理如下：
1. 想想背景如何引入（）
2. 想想每段怎么写：
	* 善用逻辑：对比、反证、举例、假设
	* 积累表达：这是比较基础的，主要是词汇的选择和复合句的使用：高级词汇 + 复杂句型（定从、同位语、倒装句）
	* 注意安排一段让步







# 阅读
## 要点：
前3min：首先看第一段，然后看每段句首，在纸上大致记下大意。后面答题时补充大意，方便答最后一题（summary）
1. TW（topic word）：
	* 全文（看标题）
	* 段落
2. EW（等价词，替换中心词）：
	* 等价词略过
	* 找新的概念（找实词）
	* 找新的逻辑关系
3. 逻辑词：
	* why（因果） 
	* 并列逻辑
